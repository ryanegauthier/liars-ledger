## Support the project

Liar's Ledger has no investors, no ads, and no data to sell. Every scan runs through real AI models and official government APIs — that costs money. Donations and Pro subscriptions are the only thing keeping the free tier alive and the daily limit high.

**[Donate on Liberapay →](https://liberapay.com/LiarsLedger)**

We accept donations through [Liberapay](https://liberapay.com) — a European, open-source, non-profit platform that processes payments via Mangopay and PayPal. We do not accept donations through Stripe or any Stripe-backed platform.

## Payment processor stance

We do not accept donations through Stripe or any Stripe-backed platform. This is a deliberate choice in opposition to Stripe's financial ties to the Israeli government and its military operations.

Pro subscriptions are currently processed through Square. We are actively researching alternatives and will migrate away from any processor with documented ties to the Israeli government or its military operations.
